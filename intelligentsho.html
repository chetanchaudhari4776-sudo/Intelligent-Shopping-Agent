FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

How does ShopAI fetch live prices?

Our agent uses official retailer APIs, affiliate data feeds, and real-time web crawlers to pull current prices every few minutes across 40+ stores.

Is my personal data or purchase history shared?

No. ShopAI never accesses your retailer accounts. Your data is encrypted at rest and never sold to third parties.

How do you make money on the free plan?

We earn a small affiliate commission from retailers when you click through and purchase. This never affects deal rankings.

Can I cancel my Pro or Team plan anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period with no cancellation fees.

Does ShopAI work outside the United States?

Currently we cover the US, UK, Canada, and Australia with store coverage expanding monthly. Currency conversion is included for all supported regions.
